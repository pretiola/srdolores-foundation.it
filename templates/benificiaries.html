Who are the beneficiaries of this project?

This project targets poor families, Children heading families (a family whereby all parents died, and the elder child takes the responsibility of a parent). Single mothers especially poor widows with children who go to school and cannot afford to have school requirements such as writing pads or books for writing from, pens and pencils. I am talking here about families whereby buying a child a shoe it is regarded as wastefulness.

What are the responsibilities?

Firstly, the beneficiaries will prepare a place where the piglets will be housed, it will be done in an ordinary manner. A start pack of feeding material and drinking bowls to be given (feeders and water etc). Having one person to visit these poor families and teaching them on how to take care of the pigs. The person will be visiting homes of beneficiaries to offer supervision services for two months. Training the beneficiaries on how to take care of the pigs in their respective homes.
