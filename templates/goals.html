What are the goals of this project?

To solve both nutrition and financial problems over 60 poor families in the village of BINZI community for their sustainability. To improve the living standard for the poor families in all spheres of life.

In summary this project will provide a start-up piglets (LOCAL BREED) to poor families without any financial means. Local breed is manageable especially to the weather climate which is tropical climate. This project of piggery is very friendly to our climate. It is also very friendly animal based.
