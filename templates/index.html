The Piglet Project

The piglet project is a kickstarter for the poor and hopeless families in the village of BINZI to become economically self-reliant, by providing them with the required material and training for engaging in piggery farming.

This project is very easy to manage especially to families that have less; in a few months they will be ready for sale. There is nothing as gratifying as being able to provide for your family. Find more information on the following pages:

  • Beneficiaries of the project
  • Challenges of the families
  • Goals of the project

Our vision

To see people of Binzi attaining holistic development and enjoying a decent livelihood through piggery farming.

Our mission

To help people see their potential and value of their surroundings, fostering food security, income, and sustainable agriculture.
