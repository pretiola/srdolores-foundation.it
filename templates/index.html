The Piglet Project

The piglet project is a kickstarter for the poor and hopeless families in the village of BINZI to become economically self-reliant, by providing them with the required material and training for engaging in piggery farming.

This project is very easy to manage especially to families that have less; in a few months they will be ready for sale. There is nothing as gratifying as being able to provide for your family. Find more information on the following pages:

  • Beneficiaries of the project
  • Challenges of the families
  • Goals of the project

Other Ways Your Support Helps the Community

Community Gardens and Plantations

Your generous support helps establish and maintain local agriculture, specifically focusing on the cultivation of bananas and coffee. These vital crops not only provide daily nourishment to keep families healthy, but the surplus produce can also be sold to generate a crucial, sustainable income.

Explore our photo gallery to see the growth:

Restoring Homes and Providing Shelter

Beyond agricultural support, your contributions are actively rebuilding the community by funding essential home repairs, structural improvements, and the finishing touches on new homes. A safe, secure, and dignified shelter is the foundation of family welfare, bringing true peace of mind to those who need it most.

Our vision

To see the people of Binzi attaining holistic development and enjoying a decent livelihood through:

  • Essential school supplies and sanitary products to help underprivileged children successfully return to the classroom
  • Access to safe and sustainable water systems
  • Pass-on-the-Gift livestock and seed programs
  • Agricultural cooperatives and capital building
  • Pastoral care rooted in Catholic social teaching

Our mission

To help people see their potential and value of their surroundings, fostering food security, income, and sustainable agriculture.
