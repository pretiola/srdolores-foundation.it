Privacy Policy

Welcome to the website of Sr. Dolores’s heart for the poor. This site is operated by Fr. Kasibante Emmanuel and friends, it is created to provide information about our works and to provide access to the information, projects, resources, products, and services available through the site to its visitors. This Privacy Policy provides guidelines to information that is collected from site visitors.

What information do we collect?

Because our website is a static and informational application hosted securely on modern cloud infrastructure (Fly.io), we do not directly collect personal data through user accounts or registration forms. However, certain technical data is collected automatically:

  • Server Logs & Infrastructure Data: Our hosting provider and application server collect standard connection logs. This includes technical information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and access times. This is standard for web servers and is used purely to maintain the security, performance, and reliability of our application.
  • Website Analytics: We use Google Analytics to better understand our visitors. This service utilizes "cookies" to collect anonymous data regarding site traffic, such as the total number of visitors to our site and the number of visitors to each page.

How does Fr. Kasibante and friends use the information they collect?

We use the information in a manner that is consistent with this privacy policy. We pool analytical information to understand how our site is being used so we can improve our outreach. Server log information is used strictly for diagnostic and security purposes. We will never sell or otherwise transfer personally identifiable information about individual users to unaffiliated third parties.

Donations and Financial Information

We rely on the generosity of people like you to fund our projects. However, our servers do not collect, process, or store any financial information. All donations are handled entirely off-site via secure third-party services (such as PayPal) or direct bank transfers (IBAN). We never have access to your credit card or direct banking credentials.

How do we protect the information we have collected?

Our website traffic is encrypted and served over a secure connection (HTTPS) to protect data transmitted between your browser and our server. Furthermore, access to our hosting infrastructure, server logs, and analytics accounts is strictly limited to authorized members.

Your access to and use of this website is subject to the Terms of Use. By accessing, browsing, or using this website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by the Terms of Use. If you do not consent to the Terms of Use, do not use this website. Fr. Kasibante and friends reserves the right to update or modify this privacy policy at any time and from time to time without notice to its users. Please review this policy periodically, and especially before you provide any personally identifiable information. Your continued use of the website after any changes to this privacy policy shall indicate your agreement with the terms of the revised privacy policy.
