Who we are

We are friends and family of Sr. Dolores who has inspired us by her spirit of serving those in need. She helped me become a Catholic priest and many other people. Let us make her spirit alive among the suffering people in the society.

Who is Sr. Dolores? She was born in Forst, that is Germany, and she became a catholic Nun in the congregation of the Holy Childhood in Oberzell. In 1955 she left Germany for a missionary assignment in Africa, where she worked for 60 years. She returned to eternity on the 25th of February 2022.
